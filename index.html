Company's Brief History

Linear Cost Consultants Ltd was founded in the year 2007 and commenced full operations in the year 2009.

It is a firm of professional quantity surveyors and construction project managers. Our services include feasibility studies, cost planning and control, tender documentation (BoQs, specifications, etc), contract administration,construction project management and auditing ongoing and finished projects.

With more than 10 years in the industry, we have proven track record of meeting the toughest challenge and finding cost-effective solutions to complex technical problems.
